Process Description
For the online process to change the name on your Universal Account Number (UAN) to ensure hassle-free PF transactions,

Follow these steps:
· Go to the UAN portal
· Login to EPFO portal
· Go to ‘Manage’
· Go to ‘Modify Basic Details’
· Now enter you’re the new or corrected name or surname
· Enter the name/surname you want to update in the UAN
· Click on ‘Update’
